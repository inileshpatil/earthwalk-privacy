## 🚀 Features

| Feature | Description |
|---|---|
| 🦶 **Step Tracking** | Automatic step counting using your phone's built-in pedometer |
| 🌍 **Earth Journey** | See how far you've walked around the circumference of the Earth |
| 🪐 **Planet Goals** | Set cosmic goals — walk to the Moon, Mars, Jupiter and beyond |
| 🍽️ **Nutrition Tracker** | Log meals from 75+ foods and track daily calorie intake |
| 🔥 **Calorie Balance** | See calories burned vs consumed with real-time deficit tracking |
| 📊 **History Charts** | Beautiful weekly and monthly step charts with streaks |
| ⚖️ **Weight Goals** | Set a target weight and track your daily burn progress |
| 🔔 **Daily Reminders** | Optional step reminders to keep your streak alive |
| 🌙 **Splash Screen** | Stunning full-screen launch experience |

## 🏃 How It Works

1. **Set up your profile** — Enter your name, age, height, weight and step goal
2. **Walk!** — The app automatically counts your steps in the background
3. **Track your journey** — Watch your total distance grow towards Earth's circumference (40,075 km)
4. **Reach the stars** — Unlock cosmic milestones: Moon (384,400 km), Mars (78M km) and beyond
5. **Log your meals** — Track what you eat and see your net calorie balance in real time

## 🛠️ Tech Stack

- **Framework:** Flutter 3.x (Dart)
- **State Management:** Riverpod 2.x
- **Local Database:** Isar (offline-first, all data stays on your device)
- **Navigation:** GoRouter
- **Charts:** fl_chart
- **Step Sensor:** Pedometer 4.x
- **Notifications:** flutter_local_notifications

## 🔒 Privacy

EarthWalk is **100% offline**. All your data is stored locally on your device only.
